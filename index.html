The LeTS (Learn Through Stories) Foundation is a not-for-profit educational venture that grew out of IDC School of Design, IIT Bombay.

It is a collaboration between design professionals and young students from far-flung parts of India. Students bring us stories that they have heard growing up.

Together, we create books.

We teach them storyboarding, illustration and book design through four- to five-day workshops that bring their stories to life. We translate these stories to English, which then become the aid to learning the language. The act of producing the book fundamentally changes learners and their relationship with learning.

Our Books

Kids love these gorgeously produced books. The rich, thick paper, the saturated colours, and the stories that seem both strange and familiar, are theirs. It is their names on the book jacket and crowding the credits page.

Their eyes and minds become sensitized to quality production of their own work, so that it is not just words, but worlds they create anew.
