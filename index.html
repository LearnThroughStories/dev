The LeTS (Learn Through Stories) project is an initiative of IDC School of Design, IIT Bombay.

It is a collaboration between illustrators, authors, animators. Some are younger than fifteen, others are design professionals. The kids are from village schools of India, and not always exposed to English-oriented education.

We invite them to narrate stories in their own language that they have heard growing up. We teach them to storyboard and illustrate select stories. These stories are translated by us into English and converted into gorgeously produced books, animated films, and mobile apps.
When the printed books reach the school libraries, the kids are already intimately familiar with the story, narrative and illustration. The English translation is the only newness. So it is that they learn from content that they themselves create!

Our Books

Kids love these books. The rich, thick paper, the saturated colours, and the stories that seem both strange and familiar, are theirs. It is their names on the book jacket and crowding the credits page.

Their eyes and minds become sensitized to quality production of their own work, so it is not just words, but worlds they can create anew.
