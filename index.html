The LeTS (Learn Through Stories) project is an initiative of IDC School of Design, IIT Bombay.

It is a collaboration between illustrators, authors, animators, some younger than fifteen and others, design professionals. The kids are from village schools of India, often not exposed to English-oriented education.

They narrate in their own language stories that they have heard growing up. We teach them storyboarding and illustration and book design. We translate their stories into English, then transform them into gorgeously produced books, animated films, and mobile apps.

Our Books

Kids love these books. The rich, thick paper, the saturated colours, and the stories that seem both strange and familiar, are theirs. It is their names on the book jacket and crowding the credits page.

Their eyes and minds become sensitized to quality production of their own work, so that it is not just words, but worlds they create anew.
